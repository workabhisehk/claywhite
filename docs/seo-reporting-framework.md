# SEO Reporting Framework

## Overview
Monthly and quarterly reporting framework to track SEO performance, measure progress toward goals (lead generation, brand visibility, traffic growth, conversion improvement), and inform strategy adjustments.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Primary Goals
1. **Qualified Lead Generation**
   - Contact form submissions from organic traffic
   - Calendly bookings from organic traffic
   - CTA clicks from organic traffic
   - Lead quality score (if trackable)

2. **Brand Visibility**
   - Branded search volume (e.g., "WH Advisory Services")
   - Brand mention tracking
   - LinkedIn profile views and engagement
   - Direct traffic growth

3. **Organic Traffic Growth**
   - Total organic sessions
   - Organic traffic by channel (Google, Bing, etc.)
   - New vs returning visitors
   - Traffic by device (desktop, mobile, tablet)

4. **Conversion Rate Improvement**
   - Overall conversion rate from organic traffic
   - Conversion rate by landing page
   - Conversion rate by keyword cluster
   - Time to conversion

### Secondary Metrics
- Keyword rankings (core + local)
- Backlink growth
- Domain Authority (DA) / Domain Rating (DR)
- Page speed and Core Web Vitals
- Indexing status
- Click-through rate (CTR) from search results

## Reporting Tools & Setup

### Google Analytics 4
- **Setup:** Already configured via Analytics component
- **Key Reports:**
  - Acquisition → Traffic acquisition
  - Engagement → Pages and screens
  - Conversions → Events (contact form, CTA clicks)
  - Custom reports for keyword clusters

### Google Search Console
- **Setup:** Verify property in Search Console
- **Key Reports:**
  - Performance (impressions, clicks, CTR, position)
  - Coverage (indexing status)
  - Core Web Vitals
  - Mobile usability

### LinkedIn Analytics
- **Metrics:** Profile views, post engagement, connection requests
- **Export:** Monthly data for reporting

### Backlink Tools
- **Ahrefs or Moz:** Track backlink growth, Domain Authority
- **Google Search Console:** Referral links report

### Keyword Tracking
- **Google Search Console:** Search performance report
- **Ahrefs or SEMrush:** Keyword position tracking
- **Google Analytics:** Organic search keywords (if available)

## Success Benchmarks

### Month 1-3 (Foundation)
- **Traffic:** Establish baseline, 10-20% growth
- **Conversions:** 2-5 contact form submissions/month
- **Keywords:** 20-30 keywords ranking in top 100
- **Backlinks:** 5-10 quality backlinks

### Month 4-6 (Growth)
- **Traffic:** 30-50% growth vs baseline
- **Conversions:** 5-10 contact form submissions/month
- **Keywords:** 50+ keywords ranking, 5-10 in top 10
- **Backlinks:** 20-30 quality backlinks

### Month 7-12 (Scale)
- **Traffic:** 100%+ growth vs baseline
- **Conversions:** 10-20 contact form submissions/month
- **Keywords:** 100+ keywords ranking, 20+ in top 10
- **Backlinks:** 50+ quality backlinks
