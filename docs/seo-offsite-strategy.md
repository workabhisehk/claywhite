## Backlink Building Strategy

### High-Value Backlink Targets

#### Resource Pages
- Compliance resource pages
- GRC framework resource pages
- Fractional executive resource pages
- Startup resource pages

#### Industry Roundups
- "Top Compliance Consultants" lists
- "Best Fractional Executive Services" lists
- "GRC Resources" lists

#### Case Study Pages
- Client case studies (with permission)
- Industry case study aggregators

### Backlink Acquisition Tactics
1. **Resource Page Outreach**
   - Identify resource pages in compliance/GRC space
   - Reach out with valuable content to add
   - Offer to contribute expertise

2. **Broken Link Building**
   - Find broken links on relevant sites
   - Offer replacement content
   - Request backlink

3. **Expert Roundups**
   - Participate in industry expert roundups
   - Provide quotes/insights
   - Earn backlinks from roundup posts

4. **Testimonials & Reviews**
   - Provide testimonials for tools/services used
   - Request backlink in testimonial
   - Review relevant software/services

## Testimonials & Case Studies

### Client Testimonials Strategy
- **Collection:** Request testimonials after successful engagements
- **Format:** Written testimonials, video testimonials (if possible)
- **Placement:** Website, Google Business Profile, LinkedIn
- **Backlinks:** If clients have websites, request testimonial page backlinks

### Case Study Development
- **Format:** Anonymized case studies (protect client confidentiality)
- **Topics:** SOC 2 readiness, investor readiness, fractional leadership ROI
- **Distribution:** Website, LinkedIn, guest posts, industry publications
- **Backlinks:** Include in guest posts, share in industry groups
