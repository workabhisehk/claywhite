## Content Structure

### Blog Post Template
1. **Title:** Keyword-optimized, compelling headline
2. **Meta Description:** 150-160 characters with primary keyword
3. **Introduction:** Hook + problem statement + value proposition
4. **Body:** 
   - H2/H3 structure with keyword variations
   - Practical examples and case studies
   - Actionable checklists or frameworks
   - Internal links to service pages
5. **Conclusion:** CTA to schedule strategy audit
6. **Related Resources:** Links to other content and service pages

### Content Optimization Checklist
- [ ] Primary keyword in title (H1)
- [ ] Secondary keywords in H2/H3 headings
- [ ] 1,500-2,500 words for pillar content
- [ ] Internal links to service pages (3-5 per post)
- [ ] External links to authoritative sources (2-3 per post)
- [ ] Local mentions (Dallas, Orange County) where relevant
- [ ] Schema markup for Article/BlogPosting
- [ ] Featured image with alt text
- [ ] Social sharing optimization

## LinkedIn Repurposing Strategy

### Weekly LinkedIn Content Plan
- **Monday:** Share blog post with key insights (carousel or article link)
- **Wednesday:** Thought leadership post on compliance trends
- **Friday:** Case study snippet or testimonial highlight

### Repurposing Workflow
1. **Extract Key Points:** Pull 3-5 main insights from each blog post
2. **Create Carousel:** Visual summary of key points (Canva template)
3. **Write Hook:** LinkedIn post with compelling hook + link to full article
4. **Engage:** Respond to comments, tag relevant companies/people
5. **Repost:** Share in relevant LinkedIn groups (GRC, compliance, fractional executives)

### LinkedIn Post Templates

**Template 1: Blog Post Share**
```
[Compelling hook/question]

Just published a guide on [topic] that [target audience] needs to know:

[3-4 bullet points of key insights]

Read the full guide: [link]

What's your experience with [topic]? Comment below.

#GRC #Compliance #FractionalLeadership
```

**Template 2: Thought Leadership**
```
[Industry observation or trend]

I've seen [problem] at [X companies] over [timeframe]. Here's what I learned:

[3 insights with examples]

The companies that succeed are the ones that [key takeaway].

What's your take? Let's discuss in the comments.

#GRC #Compliance #Leadership
```
